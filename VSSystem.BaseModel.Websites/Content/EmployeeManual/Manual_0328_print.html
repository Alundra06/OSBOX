How to use "2014 Main business list. xlsx"

Location: F:/14/2014 Main business list.xlsx

Purpose: This document is used to record customers' main information.

Frequency: Open this file when start to work.

 

This form is listed in alphabetical order basing on the column B "Business"

The column "ID COID" consists two part: 2 letters& 4 digit number.

2 letters

Shortcut   Description

CO       Carryout

DC       Dry Cleaners

GR       Grocery

GM     General Merchandise

LQ       Liquor Stores

RS       Restaurant

QT       Other

SV       Services

CL       Clothes

WH     Warehouse

PR       Professional

4 digit number

The first number of the 4 digit number implies the type of the client, as in the column "ENTITY":

1 means C- corporation

2 means S- corporation (small corp.)

3 means Sole (individual business registration)

5 means LLC (limited liability corp. (between Sole and corp.)

 

For example: LQ 1050 means it's a liquor store corporation.

                    LO means it's a Liquor Store, the first number 1 in 1050 means it's a corporation.

 

The column "Status" indicates the status of this client :

 N - New

  S - Sold

  C - Closed -> whole row will be in gray

 T - Transferred

 The column "FIN #" means Federal Tax Number, which is a 9 digits number. Used for Tax Return.

 The column "CR#" means Company registration number, which is used for sale tax.(Trader's license application has   this number, too, under the control No.)

 The column "Div" implies whose client this one is:

 S means "Mr. Lee"

SK means "Mr. Kang"

J means "Ms. Moon"

Blank means either Mr. Kim or Mr. H. Lee

 

The column "Billing" means the billing period:

 M means this client is billed monthly

Q means this client is billed quarterly


The column "Legal name" means business name is on their sign board

Businesses in Maryland are required to collect Maryland's 6 percent sales tax and or 9 percent alcoholic beverage tax.

Target group: The business in Maryland

Rate: 6 percent or 9 percent alcoholic beverage tax

Period: Monthly

Due date: The 20th of the following month- with 1.2% discount

Below is a checklist of requirements needed to obtain a Food License:

  • Item 1 : Completed Food License Application.
  • Item 2 : Approval notice from a Health Department environmental sanitarian.
  • Item 3 : Approved All Food Service Plan which must include:
  • Menu
  • Hazard Analysis Critical Control Point (HACCP) Plan (only applicable to high and moderate priority food facilities)
  • Food Service Manager's Certificate

(Food Managers must be certified within 90 days of employment; only applicable to high and   moderate priority food facilities.)

    • *  The items listed above as part of the All Food Service Plan are only required for Moderate and High Priority Facilities.
  • Item 4 : Original contract with a licensed pest control company.
  • Item 5 : Certified copy of the Articles of Incorporation if owner is a corporation.

It should have the charter/trade name from the State Department of Assessments and Taxation

located at 301 W. Preston St., Room 809, Baltimore, MD 21201 (Phone: 410-767-1330)

  • Item 6 : Workman's Compensation Insurance information. 
  • Item 7 : Any person representing the owners when obtaining the food license must bring a notarized Power of Attorney.
  • Item 8 : All handwritten documents should be notarized (not including menu and list of food equipment).
